Accommodations

Cabins

We are excited to offer you a true camp experience for our wedding weekend, with cabin accomodations! Staying at camp is a great way to be in the center of the festivities and avoid having to drive back and forth from a hotel.

For those who chose to stay in cabins, we are asking for $50/person/night to help with the cost of renting a venue where everyone can stay.

All cabins have bathrooms with sinks, mirrors, toilets, and showers. Additional showers are provided in separate shower houses.

Camp beds are single “cot sized” beds, and there are no bunks. Linens including a pillow and blanket will be provided.

Marcus and Sarah will put together cabin assignments after RSVPs. Expect to share your cabin with other friends and family!
