    What to Wear for the Ceremony

    Dressy Casual (e.g. sundresses, chinos, your nicest pair of jeans). Shoes should be appropriate for walking around outdoors.

    What to Bring

    We'll be planning some events to take advantage of Kohut's beautiful sporting facilities! Check back for more details, but plan to bring athletic and swimming apparel. September in Maine can have unpredictable weather, so make sure to bring warm clothes for evenings by the campfire.

    Some Rules

    Please respect our contract with Kamp Kohut by adhering to the following rules:

    • No dogs or other pets
    • No fireworks
    • No smoking on premises
    • No firearms
    • No underage drinking
    • Refrain from swimming when there is no lifeguard present
